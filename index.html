Key Terminology

Diagnostic evaluation
A verification-focused assessment that identifies the source, scope, and contributing conditions of a plumbing problem before repair decisions are made.
Isolation testing
A method of narrowing a problem area by controlling valves or segments so symptoms can be observed under more specific conditions.
Material transition
A junction where piping materials change, requiring compatible fittings and installation practices to reduce leakage and stress-related failure.
Inspection readiness
Work planning and documentation practices intended to align installations and repairs with permitting and inspection expectations.
Freeze-risk exposure
Conditions where piping is vulnerable to cold-driven damage, commonly linked to unconditioned spaces, exterior routing, or insufficient protection measures.
